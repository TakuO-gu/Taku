Sakuji Works

Production period December 8, 2020-present

This is an activity that takes place in the worldwide community of designers and artists to create 36 letters. I choose a different theme for each letter, and each time I learn a new way to express myself in a new form. It is easy to just look at a work of art and critique it, but it is only when I try to make it myself that I can see how interesting it is.
