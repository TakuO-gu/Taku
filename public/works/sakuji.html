Sakuji Works

Production period December 8, 2020-present

The purpose of this activity is to discover a new form of visual communication by treating letters not as symbols that convey information, but rather by treating their contours as subjects and adding design to them. By replacing the perception of letters with pure visual information, rather than relying on the knowledge context, new forms of emotion and expression can be found. Through this activity, the beauty, balance, expression, and character of the curves of existing letters have been studied.
