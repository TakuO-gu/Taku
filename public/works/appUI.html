App UI

Production period June, 2023

I designed an app UI for a unique e-commerce brand, targeting female creators in their thirties with a passion for art. Utilizing two distinct fonts—one curvaceously elegant and the other simply balanced—I chose vibrant asagi and purple hues to resonate with the user's artistic sensibilities, using gray to accentuate the asagi. The interface features striking graphics, diverging from typical luxury aesthetics to meet a market need for originality without sacrificing user experience. Beyond standard e-commerce, the app integrates a special feature showcasing creators' art and fashion, catering to users' desires to emulate admired individuals' styles, reminiscent of fashion magazine culture.
